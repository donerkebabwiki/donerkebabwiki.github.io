  • ROME

“This place is amazing! I’m so glad I found it. Owned by two friendly brothers. We traveled around in Rome trying to find a decent meal without being overpriced. We found this place because we know Turkish cuisine has a lot to offer and boy oh boy was it good. Two huge meals with drinks and snacks on the side cost us a mere 13€! Definitely recommended if you want good food and a good price.”
