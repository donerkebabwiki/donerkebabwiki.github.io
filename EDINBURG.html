  • EDINBURG

“What a joy. I was being grumpy and my daughter needed a pee so we stopped in for chips. We had great chips and then the lovely man popped out the shop having friendly banter with my daughter as he went past. He came back and gave her a sweetie. She was delighted, I was cheered up and well fed from our large portions. I will be back.”
